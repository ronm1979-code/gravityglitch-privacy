Gravity Glitch - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

Gravity Glitch ("the Game") is developed by SwanAngel. This privacy policy explains how the Game handles user data.

Data Collection

Gravity Glitch does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The Game:

  • Does not require an internet connection to play
  • Does not collect personal information (name, email, location, etc.)
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not share any data with third parties

Local Storage

The Game stores game progress (such as high scores and settings) locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the Game.

In-App Purchases

The Game may offer optional in-app purchases through Google Play. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect any personal data from children or any other users. Since no data is collected, no special provisions for children's data are necessary.

Permissions

The Game only requests the VIBRATE permission for haptic feedback during gameplay. No other permissions are required.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
