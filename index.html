Gravity Glitch - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

Gravity Glitch ("the Game") is developed by SwanAngel. This privacy policy explains how the Game handles user data.

Data Collection

Gravity Glitch does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The Game does not collect personal information such as name, email, or location.

Advertising

The Game displays optional rewarded ads provided by Google AdMob. When you choose to watch an ad, AdMob may collect the following data:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to this data.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings under Settings > Google > Ads. You may also remove ads entirely by purchasing the 50,000 crystal pack within the Game.

Local Storage

The Game stores game progress (such as high scores and settings) locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the Game.

In-App Purchases

The Game offers optional in-app purchases through Google Play. All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information.

Permissions

The Game requests the following permissions:

  • VIBRATE - for haptic feedback during gameplay
  • INTERNET - required for displaying ads and processing in-app purchases
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE - to check network availability before loading ads

Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect any personal data from children. Ad content is managed by Google AdMob, which may apply age-appropriate ad policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
